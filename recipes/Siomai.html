Siomai

Description

Siomai is a steamed dumpling originating from Chinese cuisine, widely popular across Southeast Asia and the Philippines, often served as a dim sum snack or light meal.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • wrapper

Steps and Prep

  1. Mince the carrot, onion, mushrooms, and shrimp. Place all minced ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  2. Add the ground pork, sesame oil, salt, pepper, and egg. Mix until all ingredients are well blended
  3. Scoop 1 to 1.5 tablespoons of the mixture and place it in the center of a wonton wrapper.
  4. Using your thumb and index finger, gather the edges of the wrapper around the filling, pleating as you go, while leaving the top open
  5. SSteam over boiling water for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the filling is cooked through and the wrappers are translucent and tender
